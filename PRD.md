# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## LaneShark Bowling: Premium Edition

### 1. Product Vision

**LaneShark** is a premium, web-based arcade bowling experience that merges retro aesthetics with modern, physics-based gameplay. It aims to provide the depth of a simulation (oil patterns, ball coverstocks) with the accessibility and visual flair of an arcade game.

### 2. Target Audience

- **Retro Gamers**: Fans of NES/SNES era sports games.
- **Casual Players**: Mobile and web users looking for quick, satisfying loops.
- **Bowling Enthusiasts**: Players who appreciate details like hook potential, oil patterns, and split conversions.

### 3. Core Features

- **Physics Engine**: Custom-built engine simulating hook, friction, pin action, and lane conditions.
- **RPG Progression**:
  - **XP System**: Level up to unlock items.
  - **Economy**: Earn "cash" to buy gear.
  - **Stats**: Upgradeable attributes (Power, Spin, Accuracy).
- **Pro Shop**:
  - **Balls**: Plastic (straight), Urethane (smooth arc), Resin (sharp backend).
  - **Cosmetics**: Avatars and outfits.
- **Modes**:
  - **Solo/Practice**: Grind for XP.
  - **Versus CPU**: Challenge AI personalities.
  - **Wager Matches**: High-risk, high-reward.

### 4. Technical Constraints

- **Platform**: Web (Desktop & Mobile).
- **Input**: Mouse/Touch (Swipe to throw) or 3-Click/Spacebar system.
- **Performance**: Must run at 60fps on average mobile devices.

### 5. Success Metrics

- **Engagement**: Average session time > 5 minutes.
- **Retention**: Players returning for "Daily Challenges".
- **Progression**: 50% of players reaching Level 5.

**LaneShark** is a high-octane, physics-based arcade bowling experience built for the modern web. Combining retro pixel art aesthetics with deep RPG progression systems, it offers a unique spin on the classic sport.

## ğŸ® Game Features

- **Deep Physics Engine**: Realistic pin collision, ball weights, spin mechanics, and lane oil conditions.
- **RPG Progression**: Earn XP and cash for every strike and spare. Level up your profile to unlock new items.
- **Pro Shop**: Spend your winnings on high-performance balls (Urethane, Resin, Magma) and custom outfits.
- **Multiple Game Modes**:
  - **Championship Tour**: Climb the ranks against increasingly difficult AI opponents.
  - **Wager Matches**: Bet your hard-earned cash on your skills.
  - **Versus**: Challenge a friend or the CPU.
- **Retro Aesthetics**: Immersive CRT scanline overlays, neon glow effects, and a pumping soundtrack.
